Planning Permission for Garage Conversions

Many garage conversions can be completed without full planning permission, particularly when external alterations are minimal. However, some projects may require planning approval depending on the property's location, previous planning restrictions, or the scale of the proposed changes.

As part of our service, we'll advise whether your garage conversion falls under Permitted Development rights or requires a planning application.

We regularly prepare garage conversion planning drawings for homeowners throughout York and understand the factors local planning authorities consider when assessing proposals.
