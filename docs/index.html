HOW WE CAN HELP

Whether you're planning a house extension in York, a full renovation, or a completely new build, our experienced team provides a complete design service tailored to your needs. We understand that every property is different, which is why we take the time to understand your goals, lifestyle, and budget before developing a design solution.

Our services include:

  • Planning drawings

    Our standard drawing sets provide a complete foundation for your project, featuring precision-scaled existing and proposed floor plans and elevations at 1:50 or 1:100 @A3.
    To ensure you have everything required for a formal planning application, we also offer Location and Site Plans as an optional add-on.
    We deliver your completed architectural set within 14 days of your site survey, with an express service available for projects requiring a faster turnaround.

  • House extension design (single-storey and double-storey)
  • Loft conversions
  • Internal reconfigurations
  • Garage conversions and outbuilding design
  • Full renovation and remodelling plans
  • New build architectural design
  • Interior design and space planning

    Interior Design and Space Planning: In addition to architectural services, we offer interior design in York to help you make the most of your space. Whether you're redesigning a single room or transforming an entire property, we create interiors that are both functional and visually cohesive. We believe good design should improve how you live day to day. That’s why we focus on layout, flow, and usability, as well as finishes and aesthetics. The result is a space that not only looks great but also works effortlessly for your lifestyle.

  • Planning application support

    Navigating the planning process can feel complex, but we aim to make it as straightforward as possible. We produce detailed, compliant planning drawings for York and the surrounding areas, giving your project the best chance of approval. Our team has extensive experience working with local planning authorities and understands exactly what is required for a successful application.
    We provide a flexible level of support tailored to your needs—from preparing your initial drawings to providing practical advice on local policies. For clients who would prefer not to handle the paperwork themselves, we offer a full submission service where we manage the entire application process on your behalf, ensuring every detail is handled professionally from start to finish. By choosing York Drawing Services, you benefit from clear communication, expert guidance, and architectural drawings designed to bridge the gap between your vision and a successful planning outcome.

Each project is carefully considered to ensure it enhances both the functionality and value of your property.
