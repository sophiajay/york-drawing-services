PROJECT DETAILS

Our client had a spacious garden with an unused area set away from the main house. They wanted to transform this space into a family-friendly retreat featuring ample windows to showcase and enjoy the surrounding landscape.

LOCATION: York Village
DATE: 2025

Our Process

We began by carrying out a detailed site survey to understand the topography and the existing landscape features of the garden. Using this, we developed a design that brings in plenty of natural light and creates strong visual connections to the landscape through large, slim-frame aluminium windows and doors. We incorporated sustainable materials and a rainwater-harvesting system to integrate the project with its surroundings. The design also includes an undercover BBQ area, providing a practical and sheltered outdoor space for year-round use.
